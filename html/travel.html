Sireta travel offers travelers a deep explorer local culture in Guinee, like the detailed history and story behind local characters, language, craft and religion. Our website focused on highlighting exciting,unique and memorable experiences in Guinee.We provide courses to travelers to study culture,folklore, language, and religion. The travellers also have opportunities to design and make stuff by themselves, like spell their name in local characters then put it on a t-shirt, cup , hand made craft etc. We provide crafted packages for customers who don't have time on researching, all information should be easily accessible from our site. At the beginning stage, the Sireta culture trip africa concentrates provide service in the Guinee, then it will be scaled to the west of Africa.

Target Market

Potential users of our product are travelers who are looking for verified, unique and interesting things to in Africa. Typical users will be looking for suggestions and don’t have time to spend on research.Being able to save and share trips and itinerary with their travel partners is important.

User Research

Starting my research with surveymonkey.com and involve.me, I gathered useful information like age of travelers, what they want to do when traveling, what’s the equipment they prefer to use when they plan to travel( pad, desktop, mobile ), prefer classic sightseeing activities or unique trips.

Question “ age of travelers ” will effect design system like font size, color and contrast , question “what’s the equipment they prefer to use when they plan to travel ” indicate the design for mobile first or desktop first, the result of question”prefer classic sightseeing activities or unique art trip” will affect the fate of our product.

Survey Questions:(gathered 172 responses)

  • What’s your age range?

    • Response: 20-25(40%), 25-35(35%),35-45(5%), 45-60(2%), over 60 (18%)

  • What does your typical day look like?

    • Response: 60% busy

  • what’s your employment status?

    • Response:20% students, 21% retired, other full time employee

  • Do you like to travel?If yes, Where are you most interested in traveling to next?

    • Response: 81% responses like travel, 63% choose africa.

  • Would you mind sharing with us the kinds of experiences and activities you like to do when traveling?

    • Response:93% like to share

  • What kind of equipment you use often when you plan your travel?

    • Response:57% choose desktop, 26% choose pad, other mobile

  • How likely are you to plan your next trip in Guinee? 100%,80%,60%,50%,40%......

    • Response: 70% choose 60%

  • If you plan to go to Guinee, which one do you like the most? classic sightseeing activities, unique trip

    • Response:40% choose classic sightseeing activity, 35% choose unique trip, other not sure

  • How do you prefer sharing your travel experiences and memories?

    • Response: 83% choose social network

  • How do you use your mobile phone for travel?

    • Response: 95% take photo, 60% use mobile App

Overall the survey gave me useful answers that support us to continue our project.
